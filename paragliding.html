Paragliding

Paragliding in Jammu and Kashmir is a popular adventure sport, with prominent locations including Gulmarg, Srinagar (Astanmarg), Sonamarg, Pahalgam, and Patnitop. These spots offer stunning aerial views of valleys, mountains, and lakes. The best time for paragliding is typically from May to October.

Best paragliding spots Gulmarg: A premier destination known for its panoramic views and connection to the Gulmarg Gondola. Srinagar (Astanmarg): Offers flights with views of the serene Dal Lake and surrounding hills. Sonamarg: Provides views of snow-capped peaks and valleys. Pahalgam: Known for flights over lush meadows and the Lidder River. Patnitop: A hill station on the Jammu-Srinagar National Highway, offering views of greenery and mountains. Sanasar: Located near Jammu, this area is also popular for paragliding and has training camps available. Kishtwar: A less commercialized, secluded option for those seeking a more off-the-beaten-path experience. Aru Valley: Near Pahalgam, this spot is gaining popularity and can be combined with a trek to the launch point. Best time to go The ideal period is typically from May to October. Specifically, the months of May-June and September-October are considered peak seasons, especially for locations like Sanasar. Things to consider Booking in advance: It is recommended to book your paragliding slot ahead of time, especially during peak season. Training: Facilities like training camps and equipment rentals are available in certain locations, such as Jammu and Sanasar.
