Trecking

Jammu and Kashmir offers diverse trekking options, from moderate trails like the Kashmir Great Lakes Trek and Tarsar Marsar Trek to challenging routes such as the Warwan Valley Trek. Popular treks include those that pass through alpine meadows, pristine lakes like Tulian and Pir Panjal, and high-altitude passes. The best time to visit is typically from June to September, with some exceptions like the winter Marchoi Trek

Popular treks Kashmir Great Lakes Trek: A 7-day, easy-to-moderate trek through six alpine lakes in the heart of the Himalayas. Tarsar Marsar Trek: A high-altitude trail famous for its twin alpine lakes, vibrant meadows, and spectacular mountain views. Pir Panjal Lakes Trek: Known for its unique landscapes, including numerous aquamarine lakes, vast grasslands, and opportunities to see nomadic life. Warwan Valley Trek: A moderate to difficult trek that is best undertaken in July and August.
