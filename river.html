River rafting

River rafting is popular in Jammu and Kashmir on the Lidder River in Pahalgam and the Sindh River in Sonamarg, offering options for both beginners and experts. Other locations like the Chenab River in Doda and Ladakh's Indus and Zanskar rivers also provide challenging and scenic rafting experiences, particularly for advanced rafters. The best time for rafting is generally from May to October, with August to October being ideal due to higher water flow.

Key locations for river rafting Lidder River (Pahalgam): Offers various stretches, from beginner-friendly "joy rides" to more challenging routes suitable for experts. A 2.5 km "joy ride" is perfect for beginners, while a 5 km ride is also good for novices. Sindh River (Sonamarg): Provides thrilling experiences with rapids ranging from Grade II to IV, ideal for a more adventurous trip. Offers a long 26 km stretch for those seeking a greater challenge. Chenab River (Doda, Reasi): Known for its challenging rapids, making it a great spot for experienced rafters. Rafting tours are available starting from Reasi. Indus and Zanskar Rivers (Ladakh, Kargil): The Indus River offers stretches for all levels, from easy beginner routes to professional-level rapids between Leh and Nimmu. The Zanskar River provides one of the most challenging rafting options, recommended for experts. Best time for rafting Season: The rafting season typically runs from May to October. Peak time: The months of August to October are considered the best time due to higher and faster water flow
